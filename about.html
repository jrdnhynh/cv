My Journey

To be honest, I didn't always know what I wanted to do for a career. After high school, I started out at Knox College for Business, but it wasn't the right fit, so I withdrew. I ended up at Butler Community College where I just kept "stacking" credits unitil I have three different Associate degrees. This "evolution" of my education eventually led me to Fort Hays State University for Web and Mobile Development.


Finding web dev was the "click" moment where my love for design met my interest in tech. After undergrad, I've been accepted into Georgia Tech's M.S. in Computer Science to learn even more advanced ways to build things. My goal is to keep growing as a developer and eventually create apps that are as stylish as they are functional.
